# CONTENT STRATEGY

The website should communicate trust, impact, professionalism, and inspiration.

Content should be:

- Easy to read
- Emotionally engaging
- Professional
- Action-oriented
- Authentic
- Human-centered

Avoid long paragraphs.

Prefer:

- Headlines
- Short paragraphs
- Bullet points
- Statistics
- Cards
- Visual storytelling

Use meaningful CTAs.

Write in simple English.

Be emotionally engaging without exaggeration.
