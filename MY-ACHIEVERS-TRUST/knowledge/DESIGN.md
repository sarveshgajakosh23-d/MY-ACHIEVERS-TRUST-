# Design Vision

The website should feel like a premium digital experience rather than a traditional NGO website.

The design must communicate:

- Trust
- Professionalism
- Community
- Inspiration
- Modern Technology
- Sports Energy
- Social Impact

The overall experience should be clean, elegant, and emotionally engaging.

---

# Design Style

Inspired by:

- Apple
- Nike
- Vercel
- Olympic websites
- Modern Charity Foundations

Avoid:

- Outdated NGO templates
- Cluttered layouts
- Too many colors
- Heavy gradients
- Flashy animations
- Low-quality icons

---

# Design Principles

Every page should:

- Feel spacious
- Use strong visual hierarchy
- Be easy to scan
- Guide the visitor naturally
- Have clear call-to-action buttons
- Maintain consistent spacing

The final website should feel comparable to projects built by professional design agencies.

No empty space without purpose.

No inconsistent components.

No repeated layouts.

Every page should feel polished.
